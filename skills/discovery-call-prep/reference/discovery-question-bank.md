# Discovery Question Bank

The GoWarmCRM opinionated library of discovery questions. Select 6-8 for any given call based on MEDDPICC gaps and industry.

## Pain-focused questions (use when pain is unclear or unstated)

- "Walk me through what a bad week looks like for your team right now."
- "When this problem shows up, who feels it first and how do they describe it?"
- "If nothing changes about how you handle this today, what does six months from now look like?"
- "What triggered you to start looking at this now rather than six months ago?"
- "Tell me about the last time this problem actually cost you something — what happened?"

## Metrics-focused questions (use when pain is named but not quantified)

- "How do you measure whether this is working well today?"
- "What number on your dashboard would have to move for you to call this a win?"
- "If you could fix one thing and it moved a number by 20%, which number would matter most to the business?"
- "What does your CFO or CEO ask you about related to this?"

## Champion / Economic Buyer questions (use when stakeholder picture is unclear)

- "Who else on your team lives with this problem day-to-day?"
- "Walk me through how a decision like this typically gets made at your company."
- "If we were the right fit, who else would need to be convinced?"
- "Has your team evaluated something like this before? What happened?"
- "Who owns the budget for tools in this category?"

## Decision Process questions (use when timeline and process are fuzzy)

- "If you decided today this was the right direction, what would happen next on your end?"
- "What does your typical evaluation process look like for a tool like this?"
- "Are there other initiatives competing for attention or budget in the next quarter?"

## Competition questions (use to surface alternatives including status quo)

- "What else are you looking at, including 'keep doing what we're doing'?"
- "If this doesn't work out, what's plan B for you?"
- "What would make you say no to us?"

## Industry-specific additions

### SaaS / Software companies

- "How does your team think about pipeline coverage today, and where does it fall short?"
- "What's the gap between what your CRM says and what your reps actually do?"

### Financial services

- "How does this tie into your compliance and audit requirements?"
- "What does security review look like for a tool like this at your firm?"

### Manufacturing / Industrial

- "How does this interact with your existing ERP and operations systems?"
- "What does rollout across multiple sites look like?"

### Professional services

- "How do you balance standardization with the flexibility your teams need?"

## Deal-source-specific openers

### Inbound lead

- "You filled out [form / requested demo / downloaded X] — what made you take that step now?"
- "What were you hoping to see or learn?"

### Outbound / cold reply

- "My message landed at an interesting time — what's going on that made it relevant?"

### Referral

- "[Referrer] thought this might be worth a conversation. What did they tell you about us, and what made you curious enough to say yes?"

### Event / conference

- "We met at [event]. What's stayed on your mind since?"

## Selection rules

- Pick no more than 2 questions from any single category
- Always include at least 1 Pain question unless pain is fully articulated in the payload
- Always include at least 1 Champion/Buyer question unless stakeholder picture is complete
- Match 1 question to the deal source
- If the industry has a section above and the account matches, pull 1 from there
